Classic vanilla cake

Welcome to the classic vanilla cake recipe page. Here you will find all the ingredients and steps to make a classic vanilla cake

Vanilla cakes are a classic and beloved dessert known for their light, fluffy texture and rich vanilla flavor. They serve as the perfect canvas for various toppings, fillings, and decorations, making them versatile for different occasions, from birthdays to weddings.


Ingredients

  • 21/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 21/2tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 13/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup whole milk

Key Components of Vanilla Cakes:

  • Flour: Provides structure to the cake.
  • Sugar: Sweetens and adds moisture.
  • Eggs: Help bind the ingredients together and add richness.
  • Butter/Oil: Adds moisture and tenderness.
  • Milk/Buttermilk: Moistens the batter and enhances flavor.
  • Vanilla Extract: The star flavor of the cake.
  • Baking Powder/Soda: Helps the cake rise.


Preparation:

  • Preheat the oven to 350¬∞F (180¬∞C) and grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  • In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  • In a large mixing bowl, cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  • Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  • Stir in the vanilla extract.
  • Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the milk, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients.
  • Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans and smooth the tops.
  • Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  • Let the cakes cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove and cool completely on a wire rack.
  • Once cooled, frost and decorate the cakes as desired.

    Key Steps in Making Vanilla Cakes:

    Preparation:

  1. Mixing: Creaming butter and sugar together before adding eggs, vanilla, and dry ingredients.
  2. Baking: Pouring the batter into a greased and floured cake pan and baking at a moderate temperature until golden and a toothpick comes out clean.

    3. Decorating and Frosting:

  3. Buttercream: A popular choice for frosting, made with butter, powdered sugar, and a splash of vanilla.
  4. Whipped Cream: Lighter option, perfect for a more airy feel.
  5. Fondant: For more elaborate and smooth decorations.
  6. Toppings: Fresh fruits, sprinkles, or edible flowers to add color and flavor.

Vanilla Cake Chemical Compositions:

  • Flour: Carbohydrates (mainly starch), Glutenin and Gliadin (combine to form gluten).
  • Sugar: Sucrose (C12H22O11)
  • Eggs: Albumin, Ovalbumin, and Vitellin, Fats(Mainly Lecithin), Water.
  • Butter or Oil: Triglycerides (composed of various fatty acids and glycerol), Oil: Various fatty acids (e.g., oleic acid).
  • Milk or Buttermilk: Lactose (C12H22O11), proteins (mainly Casein and Whey), and fats
  • Vanilla Extract: Vanillin (C8H8O3)
  • Baking Powder or Baking Soda: Sodium Bicarbonate (NaHCO3) combined with an acidic component, Baking Soda: Sodium Bicarbonate (NaHCO3)
